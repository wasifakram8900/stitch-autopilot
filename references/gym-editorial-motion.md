# Modern boutique fitness — editorial + motion

**The look:** magazine-editorial energy with heavy motion. Confident grid, oversized outlined
section numbers (01 / 02 / 03), generous negative space, one bold accent against a dark or
deep-charcoal base. Type does the heavy lifting — a strong grotesk/condensed display paired
with a clean body font, huge scale, tight tracking.

**Motion (the differentiator):** scroll-triggered reveals on every block, words split and rise
on the hero, sections that swap/pin on scroll, a horizontal marquee strip of disciplines
(STRENGTH · HIIT · MOBILITY · CONDITIONING), magnetic buttons, subtle parallax on hero layers.
Motion is smooth and purposeful, never gimmicky; respects prefers-reduced-motion.

**Imagery:** real action photography, monochrome-leaning with the accent popping through a
consistent duotone/gradient overlay so every shot feels like one art-directed set. Athletes in
motion, sweat, chalk, close-up equipment detail — never posed clip-art.

**Structure:** full-bleed hero → moving stat band → programs as an editorial grid with imagery →
coach spotlights → results/proof → membership pricing → schedule → bold closing CTA.

**Copy voice:** punchy, benefit-led, a little swagger. Big claims backed by numbers.

**Avoid:** centered-everything templates, pastel palettes, stocky smiling gym selfies, walls of
grey text, a hero with no photograph.
