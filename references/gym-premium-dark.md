# Premium strength gym — $10k Webflow/Awwwards caliber

**The look:** cinematic and expensive. Near-black base (#0d0f0c) with ONE electric accent
(acid lime / safety orange / blood red) used sparingly on CTAs, one hero word, and the stat
numbers. High contrast, zero pastel. Feels like a fight-club-meets-Apple product page.

**Hero:** full-bleed 100vh real photograph of an athlete mid-lift under dramatic low-key
lighting, a bottom-up black gradient scrim so text pops. MASSIVE condensed uppercase headline
(Bebas Neue / Anton / Archivo Black) bottom-left, tight negative tracking, one word in the
accent. Eyebrow shows the star rating. Two CTAs: solid accent "Start Free Session" + ghost
"View Classes". A vertical member-count stat pinned in a corner. The photo IS the hero — never
a flat colored box.

**Signature moves:** kinetic marquee of class names between sections; big animated stat
count-ups (members, PRs, classes/week, years); sticky scroll-progress rail; service/class
cards with real action images that lift + glow on hover; a dark full-width urgency band
("Claim your free week").

**Sections that sell:** trust bar → value props → programs/classes grid (image per class) →
transformation proof with big numbers → trainers grid (photos + specialty) → pricing tiers
(highlight the middle) → member testimonials with photos → weekly schedule table → urgency CTA.

**Copy voice:** imperative, short, confident. "TRAIN HARD. GET STRONG." "BUILT DIFFERENT."
Never bland ("Secure your session", "Welcome to our gym").

**Avoid:** empty gradient hero, thin/light fonts, low contrast, posed stock smiles, tiny hero type.
